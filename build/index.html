About me

Why did I wanted to become a developer?

I always wanted to create something beautiful, something special, something useful for people. But for a long time I could not find my calling. And once I saw how my friend (also a developer) was creating one site and it became very interesting to me. I began to ask him all the details, how create a wonderfull site? What is needed for this? Where do I begin? I liked everything so much that I told myself "That's what you've been looking for so long." And I began to learn programming. I chose this as my profession when entered to the university and I still like what I do.
